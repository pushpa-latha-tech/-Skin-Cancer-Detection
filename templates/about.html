About the Project

Understanding Skin cancer detection technology

Project Description

SkinGuard is a cutting-edge web-based skin cancer detection system that leverages the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyze skin lesion images and provide instant classification results. Our mission is to make preliminary skin cancer screening more accessible while emphasizing the importance of professional medical consultation.

Technology Stack

Frontend Technologies

  • HTML5 - Semantic markup and structure
  • CSS3 - Responsive design and styling
  • JavaScript (ES6+) - Interactive functionality
  • TensorFlow.js - Client-side AI model execution

Design Features

  • Fully responsive design
  • Accessibility compliant (ARIA labels)
  • Progressive Web App capabilities
  • Cross-browser compatibility

Dataset & Training

Data Source

Comprehensive collection of dermatoscopic images

Dataset Specifications

  • High-resolution skin lesion images
  • Binary classification: Malignant vs. Benign
  • Diverse demographic representation
  • Expert-validated ground truth labels

Model Architecture

Convolutional Neural Network

Our CNN model is specifically designed for medical image classification, utilizing state-of-the-art deep learning techniques to analyze skin lesion patterns, textures, and morphological features that are crucial for cancer detection.

Key Features

  • Input size: 150×150 pixel images
  • Multi-layer feature extraction
  • Dropout regularization for robustness
  • Optimized for mobile deployment

Model Layers

Input Layer: 150×150×3
Conv2D: 32 filters, 3×3 kernel
MaxPooling2D: 2×2 pool size
Conv2D: 64 filters, 3×3 kernel
MaxPooling2D: 2×2 pool size
Conv2D: 128 filters, 3×3 kernel
GlobalAvgPool: Feature aggregation
Dense: 512 units, ReLU
Dropout: 0.5 rate
Output: 2 classes (Sigmoid)

The Importance of Early Detection

Why Early Detection Matters

Skin cancer is one of the most common types of cancer worldwide, but it's also one of the most treatable when detected early. The 5-year survival rate for melanoma detected in its early stages is over 95%, compared to just 27% for advanced stages.

Statistics

  • 1 in 5 Americans will develop skin cancer
  • Early detection increases survival rates dramatically
  • Model can help identify concerning lesions
  • Regular screening saves lives

Our Role in Healthcare

SkinGuard serves as a preliminary screening tool to help individuals identify potentially concerning skin lesions. While our AI provides valuable insights, we strongly emphasize that professional medical evaluation is essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

Important Note

This web site is designed to complement, not replace, professional medical expertise. Always consult with qualified dermatologists or healthcare providers for proper diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

How to Use SkinGuard

1. Upload Image

Take a clear photo of the skin lesion and upload it to our platform. Supported formats: JPG, PNG.

2. Analysis

Our trained CNN model analyzes the image features and patterns to classify the lesion.

3. Get Results

Review the classification result and confidence score. Always consult a medical professional for diagnosis.
